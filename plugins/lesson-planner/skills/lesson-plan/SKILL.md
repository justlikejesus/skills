---
description: Build a classroom lesson plan with objectives and activities
---

Help the user produce a lesson plan. Ask for any missing inputs:
- Subject and grade level
- Topic or learning unit
- Class duration
- Available materials or constraints

Then produce:
1. Lesson title and learning objectives (measurable)
2. Materials list
3. Lesson flow with time blocks (warm-up, instruction, practice, wrap-up)
4. Differentiation notes for varied learners
5. Assessment or exit-ticket idea
