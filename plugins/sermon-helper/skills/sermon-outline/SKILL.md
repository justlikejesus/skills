---
description: Draft a sermon outline from a passage, theme, or audience
---

Help the user produce a sermon outline. Ask for any missing inputs:
- Scripture passage or theme
- Audience (e.g., Sunday service, youth group, small group)
- Length (minutes)
- Desired tone (expository, topical, devotional)

Then produce:
1. Title and one-sentence big idea
2. Opening hook
3. 2-4 main points, each with passage references and a brief application
4. Closing call-to-action and prayer prompt
